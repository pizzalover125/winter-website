Top 5 Christmas Facts

  1. Santa's reindeer names first appeared in a 1823 poem by Clement Clarke Moore.
  2. The largest Christmas tree ever was 220 feet tall, displayed in Mexico City in 2009.
  3. The first Christmas card was created in 1843 by Sir Henry Cole.
  4. NASA astronauts decorated a Christmas tree in space in 1999.
  5. Christmas is celebrated in over 160 countries, but is not a holiday everywhere.
